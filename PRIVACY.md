# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how the Palworld RESTAPI Bot ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses information about you through its Discord bot and related services (collectively, the "Services").

## 1. Information We Collect

When you use our Services, we collect the minimal data required to provide server management functionality:

- **Server Connection Data:** To enable the bot to manage your Palworld server, we collect and store your server's IP address, REST API port, and admin passwords. Passwords are encrypted at rest using symmetric encryption.
- **Discord User & Guild Data:** We collect and store Discord Server (Guild) IDs, Channel IDs, and User IDs. This is required for tracking online players, displaying dashboards, and logging moderation actions (like kicks and bans).
- **Game Data:** We temporarily fetch game data (such as player lists, server metrics, and chat) directly from your Palworld server to display it inside Discord.

## 2. How We Use the Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:
- To provide, maintain, and improve the core functionality of the bot.
- To enable Discord server administrators to manage and control their Palworld servers remotely.
- To persist moderation actions (like local bans) across game server restarts.
- To provide live-updating dashboards and voice channel player counters.
- To ensure the security and integrity of our Services.

## 3. Sharing of Information

**We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.** We may only disclose information about you:
- To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or governmental requests.
- To enforce our policies and terms of service, including investigation of potential violations.
- To protect the security or integrity of our Services.

## 4. Data Security

We take data security very seriously. All Palworld server passwords are encrypted at rest within the database. However, no database or transmission over the internet is ever 100% secure or error-free. You provide this data at your own risk.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

Data related to a specific Palworld server is kept as long as the server remains registered with the bot. If a server administrator uses the `/server remove` command, all associated data (including connection info, player logs, and local bans) is permanently deleted from the database. 

## 6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within our Services or repository.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us via our official Support Server or GitHub repository.
